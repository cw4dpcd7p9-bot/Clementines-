A South Haven Tradition

For generations, Clementine's has been the heartbeat of South Haven's dining scene. Located in the heart of downtown, just steps from the harbor and South Beach, we've been serving up hearty, home-style meals to locals and visitors alike.

Our philosophy is simple: use fresh, local ingredients, cook everything with care, and treat every guest like family. Whether you're fueling up before a day on the lake or winding down with a sunset dinner, Clementine's is your home away from home.
